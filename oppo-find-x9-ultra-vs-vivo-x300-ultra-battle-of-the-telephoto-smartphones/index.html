Oppo Find X9 Ultra vs. Vivo X300 Ultra: Battle of the telephoto smartphones

As the world of smartphones continues to evolve, manufacturers are pushing the boundaries of innovation, resulting in the development of remarkable devices that cater to the diverse needs of consumers. The Oppo Find X9 Ultra and Vivo X300 Ultra are two such flagships that have garnered significant attention due to their impressive telephoto capabilities. In this article, we’ll delve into the features, specifications, and performance of these devices to determine which one reigns supreme in the world of telephoto smartphones.

Design and Display

When it comes to design, both the Oppo Find X9 Ultra and Vivo X300 Ultra boast sleek and premium builds that exude luxury. The Oppo Find X9 Ultra features a futuristic design language, with a stainless steel frame and a glass back that provides a seamless visual experience. The device measures 163.2 x 74.7 x 8.5mm and weighs a mere 210g, making it a joy to hold and operate.

On the other hand, the Vivo X300 Ultra boasts a slightly different design approach, with a focus on minimalism and a more angular aesthetic. The device measures 163.6 x 75.4 x 9.1mm and weighs 220g, making it a tad heavier than its Oppo counterpart.

Both devices feature stunning displays, with the Oppo Find X9 Ultra sporting a 6.7-inch AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, 1440 x 3216 resolution, and HDR10+ certification. The Vivo X300 Ultra, on the other hand, boasts a 6.8-inch AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, 1440 x 3200 resolution, and HDR10 certification.

Cameras and Imaging

The Oppo Find X9 Ultra and Vivo X300 Ultra both feature advanced camera systems that cater to the needs of photography enthusiasts. The Oppo device boasts a quad-camera setup, comprising a 50MP primary sensor, a 12MP telephoto lens with 5x optical zoom, a 13MP ultra-wide-angle lens, and a 3MP depth sensor.

The Vivo X300 Ultra, on the other hand, features a quad-camera setup of its own, comprising a 50MP primary sensor, a 12MP telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom, a 16MP ultra-wide-angle lens, and a 5MP depth sensor.

While both devices offer impressive camera capabilities, the Oppo Find X9 Ultra takes the lead in terms of zoom capabilities, thanks to its 5x optical zoom lens. The Vivo X300 Ultra, however, excels in terms of ultra-wide-angle photography, thanks to its 16MP ultra-wide-angle lens.

Performance and Battery Life

Under the hood, both devices are powered by powerful processors that provide seamless performance. The Oppo Find X9 Ultra is equipped with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 processor, paired with up to 12GB of RAM and 512GB of internal storage. The Vivo X300 Ultra, on the other hand, is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8+ Gen 1 processor, paired with up to 16GB of RAM and 512GB of internal storage.

In terms of battery life, both devices offer impressive endurance, thanks to their large battery capacities. The Oppo Find X9 Ultra boasts a 4500mAh battery, while the Vivo X300 Ultra features a 4800mAh battery.

Verdict

Based on our analysis, it’s clear that both devices are exceptional in their own right, offering impressive telephoto capabilities and cutting-edge performance. However, if we had to pick a winner, the Oppo Find X9 Ultra takes the lead due to its superior zoom capabilities and premium design.

Conclusion

The Oppo Find X9 Ultra and Vivo X300 Ultra are two exceptional devices that cater to the needs of photography enthusiasts and performance enthusiasts alike. While both devices offer impressive telephoto capabilities and cutting-edge performance, the Oppo Find X9 Ultra takes the lead due to its superior zoom capabilities and premium design.
