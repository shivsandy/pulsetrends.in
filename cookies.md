  <h2>What Are Cookies</h2>
  <p>Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, analyze how you use the site, and deliver relevant advertisements. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.</p>

  <h2>Types of Cookies We Use</h2>

  <h3>Essential Cookies</h3>
  <p>These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, security, and access to secure areas. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Session cookies (temporary, deleted when you close your browser)</li>
    <li>Security cookies (help detect and prevent malicious activity)</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Advertising Cookies</h3>
  <p>We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your interests and browsing history across websites. These cookies allow Google to:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Deliver personalized advertisements relevant to your interests</li>
    <li>Limit the number of times you see a particular ad</li>
    <li>Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns</li>
    <li>Recognize when you return to our website</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Analytics Cookies</h3>
  <p>We may use analytics services to understand how visitors interact with our site. These cookies collect aggregated, anonymized information about:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Pages visited most frequently</li>
    <li>Time spent on each page</li>
    <li>How you arrived at our site (referring URL)</li>
    <li>Browser type and device information</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Cookies Set by Google AdSense</h2>
  <p>Google AdSense may set the following cookies on your device:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>_ga, _gid, _gat:</strong> Google Analytics cookies for tracking site usage (persist up to 2 years).</li>
    <li><strong>IDE:</strong> DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising (persists up to 390 days).</li>
    <li><strong>test_cookie:</strong> Used to check if the browser supports cookies (persists 15 minutes).</li>
    <li><strong>NID, ANID, DSID:</strong> Google advertising cookies for ad personalization (persist up to 13 months).</li>
  </ul>
  <p>For a complete list, refer to Google's <a href="https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cookies & Technologies</a> page.</p>

  <h2>Third-Party Cookies</h2>
  <p>Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. These include:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Google AdSense (advertising)</li>
    <li>Google Analytics (site usage analysis, if enabled)</li>
  </ul>
  <p>We do not control these third-party cookies. Please review the privacy and cookie policies of these third parties for more information.</p>

  <h2>How to Manage Cookies</h2>
  <p>You can control and manage cookies in several ways:</p>

  <h3>Browser Settings</h3>
  <p>Most web browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences through their settings. You can typically:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>View cookies stored on your device</li>
    <li>Delete individual or all cookies</li>
    <li>Block cookies from specific websites</li>
    <li>Block all third-party cookies</li>
    <li>Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is set</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Here are links to cookie management guides for popular browsers:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><a href="https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Chrome</a></li>
    <li><a href="https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enhanced-tracking-protection-firefox-desktop" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Mozilla Firefox</a></li>
    <li><a href="https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Safari</a></li>
    <li><a href="https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/view-and-delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Microsoft Edge</a></li>
  </ul>
